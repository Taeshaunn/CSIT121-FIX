<h1>My Favorite Recipe</h1>
<h2> Gail Lucas </h2>
<ul> <li>3 cups uncooked bow tie pasta </li>
  <li> 2 cups frozen peas </li>
   <li> 1 pound peeled and deviened cooked medium shrimp, tails removed </li>
   <li>1 jar (15 ounces) alfredo sauce</li>
   <li> 1/4 cup shredded Parmesan cheese</li>
</ul>
<ol>
  <li>In a Dutch oven, cook pasta according to package directions</li>
  <li> add peas during the last 3 minutes of cooking; drain and return to pan</li>
  <li>Stir in shrimp and sauce; heat through over medium heat, stirring occasionally</li>
  <li>Sprinkle with cheese.</li>
  <p> This Is my Favorite recipe because it is very simple you need few ingrediants and it is very delicious<p>
